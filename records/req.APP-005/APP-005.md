  title: "Apps MUST treat doc kinds as shared contracts and MUST NOT write outside intended doc kinds"

Because apps share a tenant-local store, shipped apps MUST treat doc kinds as shared contracts and MUST NOT perform accidental cross-doc-kind writes. Enforcement is by engineering discipline and tests, not browser sandboxing.
